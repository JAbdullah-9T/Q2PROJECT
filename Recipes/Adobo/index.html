Adobo


Ingredients (4-5 Serves)

  • 2 lbs (1 kg) chicken thighs or drumsticks (you can mix with pork if desired)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar (cane vinegar or white vinegar)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced or crushed
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns (or 1/2 tsp ground black pepper)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil/li>

Instructions

  • In a bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves.
  • Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 12 hours in the fridge for better flavor).
  • Heat the cooking oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
  • Remove the chicken from the marinade (reserve the marinade) and brown it on all sides. This step adds flavor and texture.
  • Once browned, pour the marinade into the pot.
  • Add water, black peppercorns, and sugar (if using). Stir to combine.
  • Bring the mixture to a boil, then lower the heat to a simmer.
  • Cover the pot and let it simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender. Stir occasionally.
  • Remove the lid and let the sauce reduce to your desired consistency. It should be slightly thick but not overly dry.
  • Serve hot over steamed white rice. Enjoy!
