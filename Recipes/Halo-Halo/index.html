Halo-Halo


Ingredients (4-6 Serves)

    Base Ingredients:
  • 2 cups crushed ice (or shaved ice)
  • 1/2 cup jackfruit (sliced, fresh or canned)
  • 1/2 cup sweetened saba banana (sliced, or substitute with any banana variety you like)
  • 1/2 cup sweetened red beans (mung beans or kidney beans)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened coconut strips (macapuno)
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • 1/4 cup agar jelly (gulaman, cut into cubes)
    • Toppings:
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream (or purple yam ice cream)
  • 1-2 slices leche flan (caramel custard)
  • Evaporated milk, for drizzling

Instructions

  • Fruits and Beans: If you're using fresh jackfruit, peel and slice it into strips. If using fresh saba bananas, slice them thinly. Sweeten the bananas and beans by cooking them with a bit of sugar or using store-bought sweetened versions.
  • Other Ingredients: If youâ€™re using canned or pre-sweetened coconut, nata de coco, and gulaman, drain them properly. Cut the gulaman into cubes or strips.
  • In a tall glass or bowl, layer the sweetened ingredients. Start by adding a few tablespoons of sweetened red beans at the bottom.
  • Add the sweetened jackfruit, saba bananas, and coconut strips.
  • Layer in the nata de coco, agar jelly, and palm hearts if using. You can mix and match as per your preference, but try to get a colorful mix.
  • Fill the glass with crushed ice, packing it tightly. Make sure it reaches the top of the container.
  • Add a scoop of ube ice cream (purple yam ice cream) on top.
  • Place a couple of slices of leche flan on top for a rich and creamy texture.
  • Pour a little evaporated milk over the entire dessert for added creaminess. You can add more or less milk depending on your desired consistency.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing, creamy, and flavorful layers of Halo-Halo!
  • To eat, mix all the ingredients together with a spoon or straw and enjoy the combination of textures and flavors.
