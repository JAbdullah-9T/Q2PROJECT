Leche Flan


Ingredients (6-8 Serves)

    For the Caramel:
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp water
    • For the Custard:
  • 10 large eggs
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Instructions

  • In a small, non-stick saucepan, combine 1/2 cup sugar and 2 tbsp water.
  • Heat over medium-low heat and stir until the sugar dissolves. Once the sugar is dissolved, stop stirring and let the mixture simmer.
  • Let the caramel cook until it turns into a golden amber color, being careful not to burn it. Once it's ready, carefully pour it into the bottom of a flan mold or round baking dish, swirling it to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to harden while you prepare the custard.
  • In a large bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten.
  • Add the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, 1/4 cup sugar, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth and fully combined. If desired, add a pinch of salt for added flavor.
  • Strain the custard mixture through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth into another bowl to remove any bubbles or egg solids. This will ensure a smooth and creamy flan.
  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Pour the custard mixture into the prepared mold with the caramelized sugar.
  • Prepare a water bath (bain-marie) by placing the mold in a larger baking dish. Fill the outer dish with hot water, making sure the water reaches halfway up the sides of the flan mold. This helps cook the flan gently and evenly.
  • Bake for about 45-60 minutes, or until the flan is set but still slightly jiggly in the center. You can check for doneness by inserting a toothpick or knife into the center of the flan—it should come out clean when done.
  • Once baked, remove the flan from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  • Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to let the flavors meld and the custard firm up.
  • To serve, run a knife around the edges of the flan, then carefully invert it onto a plate. The caramel will flow over the custard, creating a beautiful sauce.
  • Slice and enjoy!
