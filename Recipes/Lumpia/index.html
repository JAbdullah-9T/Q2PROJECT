Lumpia


Ingredients (Makes 20-25 rolls)

    For the Filling:
  • 1 lb (500 g) ground chicken or ground beef
  • 1 cup carrots, finely grated or minced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded finely
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
    • For the Wrapper:
  • 20-25 lumpia wrappers
  • 1 egg white or water (for sealing the wrappers)
    • For Frying:
  • Cooking oil, for deep frying

Instructions

  • In a large bowl, combine ground chicken (or beef), carrots, cabbage, onion, garlic, soy sauce, black pepper, and the beaten egg. Mix well until fully incorporated.
  • Optional: Cook a small amount of the filling in a pan to taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Separate the lumpia wrappers carefully to avoid tearing. Cover unused wrappers with a damp cloth to keep them moist.
  • Place one wrapper flat on a clean surface. Add 1-2 tablespoons of the filling near the bottom edge of the wrapper.
  • Roll the wrapper tightly around the filling, folding in the sides as you go. Seal the edge with egg white or water. Repeat until all filling is used.
  • Heat cooking oil in a deep pan or fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  • Fry the lumpia in batches, ensuring they are fully submerged in oil, for about 3-5 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove and drain on a plate lined with paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Serve the lumpia hot with dipping sauces such as sweet chili sauce, vinegar-garlic sauce, or ketchup. Enjoy!
