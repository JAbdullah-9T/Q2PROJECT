Pancit


Ingredients (4-6 Serves)

    Noodles:
  • 1 lb (500 g) rice noodles (bihon) or egg noodles
  • 4 cups water (for soaking or boiling noodles)
    • Protein:
  • 1/2 lb (250 g) chicken breast or thigh (sliced thinly)
  • 1/2 lb (250 g) shrimp (optional, peeled and deveined)
    • Vegetables:
  • 2 cups cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup green beans, sliced diagonally
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup spring onions, chopped (for garnish)
    • Sauce:
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce (halal-certified)
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth (halal-certified or homemade)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • For Cooking:
  • 2-3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 lemon or calamansi, sliced (for serving)

Instructions

  • Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 10-15 minutes, or until softened. Drain and set aside. If using egg noodles, parboil until slightly tender, then drain.
  • Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  • Add the chicken slices and cook until no longer pink. If using shrimp, add them in the last minute of cooking. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil. Saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  • Add the carrots, green beans, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes, or until slightly tender but still crisp.
  • Return the cooked chicken (and shrimp, if using) to the pan.
  • Add the softened noodles and pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chicken broth. Toss everything together to coat evenly.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, until the noodles absorb the sauce. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  • Transfer the Pancit to a serving platter.
  • Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve with lemon or calamansi wedges on the side for squeezing over the noodles. Enjoy!
